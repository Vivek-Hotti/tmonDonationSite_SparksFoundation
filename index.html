About Us

The Mumbai OpenSource Network or TMON for short, is an organization where we collaborate to make Mumbai a better place , one issue / pull request at a time. We consider ourselves to be the best platform for building community applications and solutions of all types helping Mumbaikars lead and Mumbai prosper.

MISSION

To build end-to-end products for the people of India and equiping them with digital tools to enrich their life.

VISION

To endorse a sense of opensource among budding and proffesional software and application developers; simultaneously helping the society prosper by providing extraordinary digital tools for the same.
